Our Services




Earthquake Damage Assessment / Structural Expert Witness


We provide expert witness services for structural earthquake assessments and structural defects of residential, commercial and industrial structures, leveraging our extensive experience and in-depth knowledge following 10 years of assessment following the Canterbury Earthquakes.

Our team of chartered engineers offers detailed analysis and reporting on the structural damage of buildings affected by seismic events or poor design and workmanship. We conduct thorough evaluations, to assess the damage or defect and determine the appropriate repair strategy. Whether you require support in litigation, insurance claims, or compliance with relevant codes, we deliver clear, concise, and robust assessments.


Services:

  • Structural Expert Witness
    Earthquake Damage Assessments and Construction Defects

Residential Design


We specialise in the structural design of new architectural homes and renovations, transforming architectural concepts into reality. Our team collaborates closely with architects, homeowners and builders to create buildable structural solutions. Whether designing from the ground up or enhancing an existing structure, we ensure each project receives the attention it deserves.


Services:

  • Residential
    Architectural new builds and renovations
  • Shallow Geotechnical Testing
  • Landscaping
    Retaining walls and swimming pools

Industrial and Commercial


We offer specialised structural design services for industrial and commercial structures, delivering robust and efficient solutions tailored to the unique demands of each project. Our experienced engineers work collaboratively with clients to develop solutions that meet their needs.

We provide detailed seismic analysis and earthquake damage reporting of existing Industrial and Commercial structures to determine their damage and current seismic capacity and can provide strengthening design to bring the building up to a better standard of seismic performance.


Services:

  • Commercial and Industrial
    New builds and alterations
  • Seismic Assessment Strengthening - industrial and commercial DSA
    Detailed Seismic Analysis. Seismic Strengthening
  • Structural Expert Witness
    Earthquake Damage Assessments and Construction Defects
