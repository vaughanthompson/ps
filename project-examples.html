Project Examples




Below are selected projects showcasing the range of our services. For more detail on any folio example please contact us.

Structural engineering on TC3 land


  • Industrial. Christhurch.

Project Structure were the Structural Engineers engaged as part of the Design and Build team to deliver the 5000 m2 industrial building to the client. The site was TC3 and required close collaboration between Project Structure and the geotechnical engineers.

 
 
 

Structural engineering design for a modern new build


  • Residential. Christhurch.

Project Structure provided all of the structural engineering design and drafting services for this large modern architectural home. We enjoyed working with the design and build team to make this dream home happen.

 
 
 

A challenging site + complex renovation project


  • Residential. Queenstown.

The large renovation and extension of an existing house in Queenstown required specific engineering design for all structural aspects. We enjoyed working with the homeowners to construct their dream home on this challenging site. Rendered Image Credit: AP Designs Ltd

 
 
 

A detailed engineering design for earthquake repair


  • Residential. Christhurch.

For this earthquake repair, Project Structure carried out the initial earthquake assessment, reviewed other consultants reports, and carried out the detailed engineering design for the repair. The tight site required the house to be lifted so the foundation could be replaced. Photo credit: Wiley Geotechnical Ltd
